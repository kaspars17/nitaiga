Nītaigā

Where Your Holiday Begins...

Holiday house "Nītaigā" is located in village of Saunags, in Slītere national park. If You want to get away from city, this is a perfect spot, as white sand beach is only 300 metres away, and it will not be crowded at any time of day. Built in 2018, Holiday house offers all you need for your stay, with 3 bedrooms, free wifi, bed linen, dishes in kitchen, bathroom with shover and toilet. Kids have playground on site, with sand box and swing set.Kolka cape is located 11 km away. Deer horn museum is located 3 km away.
